## 2. Data We Collect

We collect and process the following data depending on how you use the app:

### 2.1 Login and Technical Data

For the purpose of identifying and authorizing users within the app, we may process:

- Email address  
- Password (stored only in hashed form, never in plain text)
- IP address (retrieved temporarily but not stored)  
- Device information (e.g. operating system, device model)  
- App version
- Date and time of access
- Crash and error details  

This data is required to ensure secure authentication, app stability, and reliable operation.

---

### 2.2 Energy-Related Data

For the purpose of displaying energy information, performing simulations and analyses, we may process the following energy-related data:

- Metering point number(s)
- Grid operator
- Historical electricity consumption and production data

**Important notice:**  
The app does not modify or influence your energy supply or contractual conditions.  
Energy data is used solely for visualization, simulation, and calculation purposes within the app.
