Title: Browse data marketplaces, install services, and manage your data permissions.

Description: Take control of your data with the Marketplace App. It gives you a clear, transparent way to discover data services, join marketplaces, and decide exactly who can access your data.

Whether you're looking to unlock new energy services, explore data-driven applications across multiple domains, or simply stay in control of your consent preferences, the Marketplace App puts you in the driver's seat.

Key Features:
Marketplace Discovery: Browse and join energy marketplaces—with more domains like Health, Weather, Finance, and Mobility coming soon.
Service Installation: Discover and install data services directly onto your AIIDA data wallet with just a few taps.
Permission Management: Review, accept, decline, or revoke data permissions at any time—full transparency and control over your consent.
AIIDA Integration: Connect your AIIDA (Administrative Interface for In-house Data Access) device as your personal data wallet and link it to the marketplaces you join.
Marketplace Insights: Track performance metrics and revenue data for every marketplace you participate in.
Messages: Stay up to date with notifications and updates from your connected marketplaces and services.

Why Choose the Marketplace App?
The Marketplace App makes data sovereignty simple. By putting permission management and service discovery in one place, we empower you to benefit from your data—on your terms.
